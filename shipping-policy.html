Shipping Policy

At SootheTap, we strive to get your orders to you as quickly and safely as possible. Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed, we process it promptly to ensure it moves through our system without delay.

Our standard shipping time is 5–12 business days, depending on your location.

Please note that delivery times may vary due to factors beyond our control, such as postal service delays, holidays, or high-volume periods.

You will receive a confirmation email with tracking information once your order has been shipped, so you can monitor its progress every step of the way. We recommend keeping an eye on your email for updates and estimated delivery dates.

Please ensure that the shipping address you provide is accurate and complete. SootheTap is not responsible for delays or misdeliveries caused by incorrect addresses.

We appreciate your patience and understanding, and we are committed to providing you with a smooth and reliable shipping experience.
