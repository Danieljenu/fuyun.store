Returns

We stand behind every Fuyun plush we sell. If something isn't right with your order, we offer a 30-day money-back guarantee for items that are defective, damaged, or not as described.

What Can Be Returned?

We accept return requests for the following reasons:

  • The item arrived damaged or defective.
  • The wrong item was sent to you.
  • The product does not match its description on our website.

Returns are not accepted for the following:

  • Change of mind or accidental orders (please double-check your order before confirming).
  • Items showing signs of misuse, accidental damage, or normal wear and tear.
  • Shipping delays caused by carriers once the order has been dispatched.

How to Start a Return

1
Contact Us Within 30 Days Email us at omerbuisnessacc@gmail.com within 30 days of receiving your order.
2
Include Your Order Details Provide your order number, a brief description of the issue, and photos or a short video if the item is damaged.
3
We Review Your Request Our support team will review your request and respond within 1–3 business days with next steps.
4
Return & Refund Processed If approved, we will provide return instructions. Once the item is received and inspected, your refund will be issued to your original payment method.

Refund Timelines

Once your return has been approved and processed, please allow the following timeframes for your refund to appear:

  • Credit / Debit Card: 5–10 business days depending on your bank.
  • Apple Pay / Google Pay / Shop Pay: 3–5 business days.
  • Klarna: Please refer to Klarna's own refund policy for timing.

You will receive a confirmation email once your refund has been issued from our end.

Exchanges

We currently do not offer direct exchanges. If you received a defective or incorrect item and would like a replacement rather than a refund, please mention this when you contact us and we will do our best to accommodate your request.

📧 For all return enquiries, please email us at omerbuisnessacc@gmail.com with your order number and details. You can also use our Contact page.

Note: All SootheTap products are fulfilled directly by our supplier. Items are shipped only after an order is placed. Return shipping instructions will be provided on a case-by-case basis once your request is approved.
